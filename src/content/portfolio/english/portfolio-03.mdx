## Overview

Pinalog Spatial: Container is an intelligent 3D loading engine that revolutionizes freight logistics. By calculating optimal packing arrangements for varied cargo, it maximizes volumetric efficiency while ensuring load stabilityâ€”solving one of the most complex problems in global shipping.

### The Challenge

Container loading is a multidimensional optimization problem:

- **Volume Maximization**: Pack as much cargo as possible
- **Weight Distribution**: Maintain stability and safety
- **Cargo Protection**: Prevent damage during transport
- **Loading Order**: Optimize for delivery sequence
- **Constraint Handling**: Account for fragile items, weight limits, and stacking rules

Manual planning is time-consuming and suboptimal, often leaving 20-30% of container space unused.

### Our Solution

We developed a physics-based 3D engine that:

1. **3D Cargo Analysis**: Scans and models all items to be loaded
2. **Genetic Algorithm Optimization**: Tests millions of packing permutations
3. **Physics Simulation**: Validates stability and safety of each arrangement
4. **Constraint Management**: Respects all real-world loading rules
5. **Visual Loading Guide**: Generates step-by-step 3D loading instructions

### Technical Innovation

- **Custom Physics Engine**: Real-time stability calculations for complex cargo arrangements
- **Multi-objective Optimization**: Balances space utilization, weight distribution, and loading efficiency
- **Constraint Solver**: Handles dozens of simultaneous loading constraints
- **3D Visualization**: Interactive preview of optimal loading sequence

### Results

- **92% average space utilization** (industry average: 70%)
- **35% reduction** in loading time
- **Zero stability incidents** since deployment
- **$1.2M annual savings** for pilot client
- **Handles 200+ items** per container in under 3 seconds
